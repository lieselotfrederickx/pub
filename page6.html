2 BACHELOR

'Encyclopedia Vulgaris' is the title of this school assignment. I could choose what I wanted to collect and I chose sea shells. The additional text that I had to use was 'Book of the human body' and I added poems about death, because I made an association with death.

I had to make three records. They had to have a similar identity, but it were three different records. I also had to use typography on the cover. My theme was 'holes', so I carved out the letterspace, linespace and innerspace on my covers and drew them on my sleeves.
